1. Executive Summary

1.1 Project Overview

The Society Events Management Platform is a comprehensive web-based solution designed to streamline event management for residential societies, vendors, exhibitors, and administrative staff. The platform serves as a centralized hub for managing all aspects of society events, from planning and vendor coordination to billing and reporting.

1.2 Business Objectives

  • Primary Goal: Digitize and automate society event management processes
  • Secondary Goals:
    • Reduce manual paperwork and administrative overhead
    • Improve vendor-society coordination
    • Enhance revenue tracking and financial transparency
    • Provide data-driven insights for better decision making
    • Streamline exhibitor registration and management

1.3 Target Users

  • Super Administrators: Platform-wide management and oversight
  • City Administrators: Regional management and coordination
  • Support Technical Staff: User assistance and technical support
  • Sales & Marketing Teams: Lead generation and campaign management
  • Accounting Teams: Financial management and billing
  • Logistics Teams: Vendor and supply chain management
  • Society Representatives: Event planning and management
  • Vendors: Service provision and job management
  • Exhibitors: Event participation and booth management

2. Business Requirements

2.1 User Management & Authentication

2.1.1 Role-Based Access Control

Requirement: The system must support hierarchical role-based access control with the following roles:
Role Description Key Permissions
Super Admin Full system access and control User management, System configuration, Global reporting
City Admin City-level administration Local event management, Regional vendor coordination
Support Tech Technical support and assistance Event troubleshooting, System monitoring
Sales & Marketing Lead management and conversion Marketing campaigns, Customer relations
Accounting Financial management Financial reports, Invoice management
Logistics Vendor and supply management Vendor management, Supply chain oversight

2.1.2 User Authentication

  • Secure email/password authentication
  • Session management with configurable timeout
  • Password policy enforcement
  • Account lockout after failed attempts
  • Optional two-factor authentication

2.2 Society Management

2.2.1 Society Registration & Onboarding

Requirement: The system must allow registration and management of residential societies with comprehensive profile management capabilities.

Society Profile Management

  • Basic information (name, location, contact details)
  • Membership count and demographics
  • Available facilities and amenities
  • Contact person assignment
  • Status tracking (active, pending, inactive)

Facility Management

  • Auditoriums and halls
  • Outdoor spaces and gardens
  • Parking facilities
  • Technical equipment availability
  • Capacity limitations

2.3 Event Management

2.3.1 Event Lifecycle Management

Requirement: The system must support complete event lifecycle management from creation to completion.

Event Creation & Planning

  • Event details (title, description, date, time, venue)
  • Capacity management and attendee limits
  • Multi-status workflow (draft, published, ongoing, completed, cancelled)
  • Vendor assignment and coordination
  • Budget planning and tracking

Event Execution

  • Real-time attendee tracking
  • Vendor coordination and communication
  • Live event updates and notifications
  • Issue tracking and resolution

Post-Event Management

  • Revenue calculation and reporting
  • Vendor performance evaluation
  • Attendee feedback collection
  • Event analytics and insights

2.4 Vendor Management

2.4.1 Vendor Registration & Onboarding

Requirement: The system must support comprehensive vendor management across multiple service categories.

Vendor Categories

  • Sound & Lighting services
  • Catering and food services
  • Decoration and event styling
  • Security services
  • Transportation and logistics
  • Housekeeping and maintenance

Vendor Profiles

  • Company information and credentials
  • Service offerings and specializations
  • Pricing structures and packages
  • Geographic service areas
  • Performance history and ratings

2.5 Exhibitor Management

2.5.1 Exhibitor Registration

Requirement: The system must facilitate exhibitor participation in events with comprehensive registration and booth management.

Registration Process

  • Company information and contact details
  • Product/service category classification
  • Booth preferences and requirements
  • Payment processing and tracking
  • Registration status management

Booth Management

  • Booth allocation and assignment
  • Layout planning and visualization
  • Equipment and utility requirements
  • Setup and breakdown scheduling

  • Data Encryption: SSL/TLS for data in transit
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication support
  • Authorization: Role-based access control

5. Technical Architecture

5.1 Frontend Requirements

  • Framework: React.js with TypeScript
  • Styling: Tailwind CSS for responsive design
  • State Management: React hooks and context
  • Icons: Lucide React icon library

5.2 Backend Requirements

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  • Authentication: Supabase Auth
  • API: RESTful API design
  • File Storage: Supabase Storage

5.3 Infrastructure Requirements

  • Hosting: Cloud-based deployment
  • CDN: Content delivery network for static assets
  • Monitoring: Application performance monitoring
  • Backup: Automated database backups

6. Data Requirements

6.1 Core Entities

Entity Description Key Attributes
Users System users and authentication ID, Email, Role, Status, Profile Data
Societies Residential society information Name, Location, Facilities, Contact Info
Events Event details and lifecycle Title, Date, Venue, Status, Capacity
Vendors Service provider information Company, Category, Rating, Pricing
Exhibitors Exhibition participant data Company, Booth, Category, Payment Status
